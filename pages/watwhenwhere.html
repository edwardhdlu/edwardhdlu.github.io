VISIT WATWHENWHERE

WatWhenWhere is a utility webapp intended to help Waterloo students find common times in their schedules. We've made this easy - all you have to do is paste your term schedule, add your friends, and check your availabilities.

This app was built in PHP, MySQL, Javascript, CSS and HTML by myself and three friends. I worked on the frontend portion of the app, starting by designing mockups for a mobile-first interface. After a few revisions, I built the frontend statics followed by the functionality.

This was my first real "full" project in the sense in that, over a short period of about two weeks, I saw the app go from a simple idea to a complete, usable webapp that gathered over 120 users within the first few days of release.
